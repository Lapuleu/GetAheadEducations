Courses We Offer

Get Ahead focuses on STEM courses, including mathematics, science, and computer programming.

Computer Science Principles

Get Ahead offers two types of Computer Science Courses. We offer Computer Science Principles for computer beginners, as we take a look into the basics of computer hardware, and do some easy coding.

Advanced Programming

Our more advanced Computer Science course for intermediate to advanced coders focuses on tackling algorithmic problems through various coding methods.

Arithmetics

Our introductory math course, fit for children in elementary and middle school where we focus on the basics of math problems with a slight intro to algebra.

Algebra I and II

These are two separate classes, but both are tailored to school curricula with Algebra I having a focus on the basics of algebra, and Algebra II delving into more advanced functions and trigonometry.

Calculus

Our calculus program at Get Ahead focuses more on the central ideas of calculus and pre-calculus. We delve into limits and derivatives, but do not explore the more advanced concepts of calculus.

Chemistry and Biology

Two separate courses, we follow a traditional curriculum of learning from experiments and theoretical knowledge. We offer fun, at-home experiments that students can do themselves.
