Each course will be held on their respective days of the week, from 2-7 pm EST. No courses will be held at the same time. The program start June 23rd, 2025 and will continue for 9 weeks, ending August 22nd. Course times are subject to change.


Computer Science Principles - Tuesdays

Get Ahead offers two types of Computer Science Courses. We offer Computer Science Principles for computer beginners, as we take a look into the basics of computer hardware, and do some easy coding.

Advanced Programming - Wednesdays

Our more advanced Computer Science course for intermeditate to advanced coders focuses on tackling algorithmic problems through various coding methods.

Arithmetics - Mondays

Our introductory math course, fit for children in elementary and middle school where we focus on the basics of math problems with a slight intro to algebra.

Algebra I and II - Thursdays

These are two sepereate classes, but both are tailored to school curriculums with Algebra I having a focus on the basics of algebra, and Algebra II delving into more advance functions and triginometry.

Calculus - Fridays

Our calculus program at Get Ahead focuses more on the central ideas of calculus and pre-calculus. We delve into limits and derivatives, but do not go into the more advanced ideas of calculus.

Chemistry and Biology - Mondays and Tuesdays

Two seperate courses, we follow a traditional curriculum of learning from experiments and theoretical knowledge. We offer fun, at-home experiments that students can do themselves.
